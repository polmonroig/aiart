Meet aiart - your intelligent art advisor


Upload an image to see how aiart evaluates it. And don't worry! we do not store any of your images or information.

Our algorithm will see your image in the eyes of:


  • Art expert: who evaluates formal attributes of an image based on fundamental art theory of recognized experts in the field.
  • Common public: who will instantly form an opinion on the attractiveness of your image.

How it works:


  • We have researched and synthesized the fundamental principles of composition and color harmony into an algorithm.
  • We conducted a study on over 300 people to evaluate the effectiveness of our algorithm. The results of the study showed a 96% of fidelity. You can see the published paper here.

What kind of images we can process:


  • Art work of any medium as long as you have a pixel representation of it.
  • Photos of furniture, buildings, clothing attire, food. Or just about anything that has a color palette and clear points of attention.

Highly condensed images, intricate patterns, unrecognizable shapes and low contrast images will result in a poor analysis of the composition and the algorithm will not evaluate it, although it will still evaluate its color harmony.
Monochromatic images (or nearly) will not return a color harmony evaluation, - since its purpose is specifically to evaluate and improve the combination of various colors.

Who needs our service:


  • Artists of any medium: Have an impartial and precise evaluator of your work, use the aiart score to push your piece of work to the next level.
  • Graphic designers: Verify the compatibility of your color scheme and the visual organization of the elements.
  • Art directors: Rapidly take decisions on set about color and composition. Take out your camera and evaluate on real- time your surroundings.
  • Art students: You can learn the fundamental principles of composition and color harmony with immediate feedback using aiart algorithm.

How did the project start:


  • Art should not be left behind from the race of technological improvement. Artists and creatives aided by technology are undoubtedly the future.
There is a constant improvement of society towards greater accuracy and objectivity in all disciplines except art, the realm of art has always been left aside to the relative and subjective, even though gestalt psychology has proved we are wired in the same way, our experience on visual feedback is predictable and repeatable. We believe we can set up the common ground for artist to start with heads up towards improvement.

Stay tuned!


Our two-parts algorithm is just the start of a bigger project, we are planning to develop a fully capable Artificial Intelligence assistant to guide on more complex and precise areas of artistic creation.
